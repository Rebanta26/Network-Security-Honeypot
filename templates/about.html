About FakeStore

Welcome to FakeStore, established in 2025. Our mission is to create a decoy eCommerce platform to study and analyze malicious bot activities targeting online stores.

This project serves as a honeypot for network security research. We monitor automated and suspicious activities—such as rapid checkout attempts or unauthorized data scraping—to gain valuable insights into bot behavior.

Through this project, we aim to develop effective countermeasures and improve overall web security by understanding how malicious bots operate in the real world.
